What is the leading cause of deaths in the US as of 2017?

Heart disease with a total of 647,457 deaths. Over the years, heart disease including heart attacks, has been one of the leading causes of deaths in the US.

Which gender do you believe has the higher life expectancy?

Women live an average of 5 years longer than men in the US. They have a life expectancy of 81.1 years and men have a life expectancy of 76.1 years.

Which country has the highest life expectancy?

Monaco actually has the highest life expectancy of 89.4 years. The second highest is Japan with 85.3 years.

The United States is actually 43rd in the world for life expectancy with the average sitting at around 80 years. This is actually pretty good! Depending on the location you are getting your data from, it will range between 75-80 years.
